WELCOME TO THE BITE CLUB

Meet our dogs. They'd love to meet you!

We're passionate. We're non-profit. And we're volunteer-run. Although we do not exist, our mission is to rescue as many dogs as we can and find happy homes for them where they will be properly taken care of. The relationship between dogs and humans date back to centuries. Dogs and Humans share a very special connection and we are here to embrace that.
Meet some of the dogs from our bite-club. All our dog-members are very cool. You will love them.

FIND YOUR PAW-SOME FRIEND IN OUR CLUB!

Adopt your favourite dog from our club.

We have thousands of amazing dogs in our club, who need love, care and support. They need a home. If you like any of our members from The Bite Club, fill out an application for that particular member. We would be more than happy to review your application.
